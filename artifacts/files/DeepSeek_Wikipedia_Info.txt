DeepSeek is a Chinese artificial intelligence company known for developing large language models (LLMs). It was founded in Hangzhou, Zhejiang, China, in July 2023, by Liang Wenfeng. The company gained recognition for its DeepSeek-R1 model, which is reported to provide responses comparable to OpenAI's GPT-4 while being significantly cheaper to train. The company has been noted for its innovative approaches in AI, using fewer computational resources compared to its competitors. It has faced scrutiny and praise for challenging AI market leaders, highlighting its potential impact on the global AI industry.
